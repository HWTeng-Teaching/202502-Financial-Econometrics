## Ans:
```text
| x  | y  | x - x̄ | (x - x̄)^2 | y - ȳ | (x - x̄)(y - ȳ) |
|----|----|----|----|----|----|
| 3  | 4  | 2   | 4   | 2   | 4   |
| 2  | 2  | 1   | 1   | 0   | 0   |
| 1  | 3  | 0   | 0   | 1   | 0   |
| -1 | 1  | -2  | 4   | -1  | 2   |
| 0  | 0  | -1  | 1   | -2  | 2   |
| 5  | 10 |  0  | 10  |  0  | 8   |

Mean x̄ = (3+2+1-1+0)/5 = 1
Mean ȳ = (4+2+3+1+0)/5 = 2

Σxi = 5, Σyi = 10
Σ(x - x̄)^2 = 10, Σ(y - ȳ)^2 = 10, Σ(x - x̄)(y - ȳ) = 8
