## Ans:
### (a) Complete the entries in the table

| x  | y  | x - x̄ | (x - x̄)^2 | y - ȳ | (x - x̄)(y - ȳ) |
|----|----|----|----|----|----|
| 3 | 4 | 2 | 4 | 2 | 4 |
| 2 | 2 | 1 | 1 | 0 | 0 |
| 1 | 3 | 0 | 0 | 1 | 0 |
| -1 | 1 | -2 | 4 | -1 | 2 |
| 0 | 0 | -1 | 1 | -2 | 2 |
| **5** | **10** |**0**| **10** | **0**  | **8** |

### 計算過程

Mean x̄ = (3+2+1-1+0)/5 = 1
Mean ȳ = (4+2+3+1+0)/5 = 2

Σxi = 5, Σyi = 10
Σ(x - x̄)^2 = 10, Σ(y - ȳ)^2 = 10, Σ(x - x̄)(y - ȳ) = 8

### (b) 
| 參數 | 計算過程 | 結果 |
|----|----|----|
| \(b_2\) | \( \frac{\sum (x - \bar{x})(y - \bar{y})}{\sum (x - \bar{x})^2} \) | \( \frac{8}{10} = 0.8 \) |
| \(b_1\) | \( \bar{y} - b_2 \bar{x} \) | \(2 - 0.8 \times 1 = 1.2\) |
