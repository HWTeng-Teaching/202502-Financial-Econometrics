Show that for these numerical values $\bar{\hat{y}}=\bar{y}$, where $\bar{\hat{y}}=\sum\hat{y}_i/N$

### Ans

$$\bar{\hat{y}}=\sum\hat{y}_i/N=\frac{3.6+2.8+2+0.4+1.2}{5}=\frac{10}{5}=2=\bar{y}$$
