(a) Test $H_0: \beta_2 = 0$

1.Estimate under $H_0$: The quantity of interest is simply $\beta_2​$.

2.Test statistic:

t = $\frac{b_2 - 0}{\sqrt{\mathrm{Var}(b_2)}}
  = \frac{3}{\sqrt{4}}
  = \frac{3}{2}
  = 1.5.$

3.Decision:
$|1.5|$ < 2.00 
$\quad \Longrightarrow \quad 
\text{Fail to reject } H_0.$
